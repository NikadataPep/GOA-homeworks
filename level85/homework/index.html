LEXUS RC 350F

The 2022 Lexus RC 350 F Sport is a luxury coupe powered by a 3.5L V6 engine producing 311 hp and 380 Nm of torque. With a 0-100 km/h time of around 5.8 seconds, it offers a blend of sporty handling, premium comfort, and aggressive styling, making it a thrilling grand tourer.

The BMW F90 M5, a high-performance luxury sedan, features a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 producing 600 hp (617 hp in the Competition model) and 750 Nm of torque. It accelerates from 0-100 km/h in just 3.4 seconds with xDrive AWD, making it one of the fastest sedans ever.

The BMW F80 M3, a track-ready sports sedan, is powered by a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 engine generating 425 hp (444 hp in the Competition model) and 550 Nm of torque. It sprints from 0-100 km/h in about 4.0 seconds, with precision handling and aggressive styling.
